* AspectRatio is always 16:9 so it can scale automatically on any device display.
* The camera projection is orthographic
* The icons for the reels will be full poker cards
* At the beginning will be 5 reels with 3 visible icons(play cards with the back) each
  At SPIN button press, the cards(reels icons) will spin around Y-axis a couple of times and will stop with the face up
* The pay lines will be only the rows 
* The paytable will contain the same rules as 5 cards poker(2 cards, 3 cards, full house, 4 of a kind, flush, strait flush, colour, strait colour, royal flush)
